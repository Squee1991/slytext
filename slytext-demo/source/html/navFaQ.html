What is slyText and how does it work?
SlyText is a "web text" messaging service that allows its users to send messages from the web to any smartphone in the United States or Canada, and back! SlyText allows you to send a text to a group of telephone numbers via our web browser. The recipients will receive your message on the date and time of your choice from the telephone number you choose when signing up for an account. In the interface, you will have access to all sms messages conversations, replies and messaging history.
